Impact Press

For situations where a hammer strike isn't sufficient, our Impact Press delivers the necessary pressure to get the job done. Easy and safe to operate, this manual and pneumatic impact press can be used anywhere in the factory or shop. The marking process is achieved by compressing and releasing a spring-loaded ram, ensuring efficient and precise marking on metals.

Features

  • Adjustable Marking Pressure: Quickly and easily adjusted to suit different workpieces.
  • Automatic Compensation: Adjusts for different workpiece thicknesses (note: airline pressure only compresses the spring and does not determine impact force).
  • Versatility: Ideal for light assembly work and sheet metal work, including riveting, flaring, piercing, shearing, bending, and more.
  • High Efficiency: Output is as fast as manual loading allows, with minimal rejections, even in precision jobs.
  • Long Tool/Die Life: Designed for extended tool and die lifespan.
  • Automation Ready: Can be adapted for automation with appropriate fixtures, tools, and dies.
  • Compact and Portable: Tough, versatile, and portable design.
  • Tool-Free Adjustments: Easily adjustable without the need for tools.
  • Adequate Worktable: Features tee slots for easy fitting of work-holding fixtures.
  • Low Maintenance: Simple to operate with minimal maintenance requirements.
  • Material Compatibility: Works on soft metals and mild steel.
  • Accessory Compatibility: Compatible with many accessories made by CHANDRIKA.

Machine Holder for Impact Press

When frequent changes in the marking stamp are needed, a Steel Type Holder is ideal. Our machine type holders are compatible with steel type characters of various sizes, enhancing efficiency and ensuring accurate, uniform imprints of identical depth. These holders allow you to arrange individual characters to spell out different names, codes, or serial numbers.

Features

  • Accurate Type Location: Designed for precise IST type location and alignment.
  • Positive Locking: Ensures secure locking of IST types.
  • Quick Change and High Interchangeability: Enables quick changes of IST types with high interchangeability.
  • Durability: Long-lasting construction.
