Pin Marking Machine

Pin Marking Machine

Introducing an extremely affordable and user-friendly marking machine designed to provide high-quality, permanent, and programmable marking on a wide range of materials, from soft plastics to hard metals up to 60 HRC. This versatile machine is ideal for various industries and applications, ensuring reliable and precise marking with minimal maintenance.

Features

  • Versatile Marking Capability: Can mark a variety of materials including soft plastics and hard metals.
  • Large Marking Area: Standard marking area window of 6” x 4” (150 x 100 mm).
  • Portable Model: Hand-held portable model with a marking area of 4” x 2” (100 x 50 mm).
  • High-Speed Marking: Ensures operational reliability with high-speed marking.
  • Mechanical Simplicity: Designed for ease of use and low maintenance.
  • Position Check Mode: Allows users to check all positions of line/shape starting position to prevent loss of marking plates.
  • Automatic Data Generation: Capable of automatically generating serial numbers, date, time, shift codes, QR codes, etc.
  • Geometrical and Custom Shapes: Can mark texts, data, geometrical shapes (square, circle, oval, rectangle), customized logos, and vector images.
  • Comprehensive Font Support: Supports all system fonts along with special logo fonts.
  • Lightweight: Marking head weighs approximately 4 kg, controller weighs approximately 1 kg.
  • Operational Temperature: Functions within a temperature range of 0 to 50 degrees Celsius.
  • Tungsten Carbide Pin: Durable marking pin material.
  • Adjustable Marking Speed: Marking speed can be adjusted depending on the material and character height.
  • Marking Depth: Can mark up to 0.6 mm, depending on material and hardness.
  • USB Communication: Easy connectivity via USB.
  • Compact Controller: Operable through a computer, laptop, palmtop, or tablet PC.
  • User-Friendly Software: Menu-driven software that is easy to navigate.
  • ERP Integration: Customizable solution for integrating marking software with customer’s ERP to extract and mark data.
  • Pin Stroke: Maximum pin stroke of up to 10 mm, depending on pin selection.
  • Robotic Cable Length: Standard length of 2 meters, with custom lengths available.
  • Air Pressure Requirement: Operates at 5 to 6 bar.
