Fibre Laser Marking Machine

Our Fibre Laser Marking Machine delivers high performance and precision for a variety of marking applications. It is designed to offer exceptional marking speed, quality, and different window sizes, making it suitable for a wide range of materials and industries.

Features

  • High Precision: Delivers high precision for various marking applications.
  • Multiple Power Options: Available in 20 Watts, 30 Watts, and 50 Watts.
  • Wide Range of Materials: Suitable for a wide range of materials and industries.
  • Fast Marking Speed: Marking speed up to 7000 mm/sec.
  • Compact Design: Compact and easy to integrate.
  • Maintenance Free: No optical alignment required.
  • Long Life: Expected MTTF (Diode) life greater than 100,000 hours.
  • Cooling Type: Air cooled (if required at extra cost).
  • Environment Requirements: Free from dust, oil, mist, and other contaminations.
