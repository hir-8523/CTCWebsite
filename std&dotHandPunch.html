Standard and Dotted Hand Punches

Standard Hand Punches

Our Standard Hand Punches are crafted from high-quality, heat-treated tool steel, ensuring durability and precision for various marking applications. These punches are designed to provide deep, clear, and legible stamped impressions on a wide range of metals.

Features

  • High-Quality Material: Made from high-grade tool steel, heat-treated for durability.
  • Sharp Face Stamps: Ideal for marking steel, stainless steel, precious metals, copper, brass, and bronze.
  • Durable Design: Finely balanced with a liberal overall shank size to safeguard against chipping and splitting at the hammering end.
  • Precision Heat Treatment: Hardness of 58-62 Rockwell C (HRC) at the character and 32-40 Rockwell C (HRC) at the striking end.
  • Clear Identification: Each punch is clearly marked with character designation and size.
  • Rust-Free Finish: Dull nickel plating ensures a longer shelf life.
  • Versatile Applications: Suitable for all engineering and industrial applications, can be used with regular hammers or impact presses.
  • Custom Options: Low-stress dotted hand punches, custom logo punches, reverse alphabets and numbers for forging and die casting, and butt-welded stamps for marking red-hot materials.
