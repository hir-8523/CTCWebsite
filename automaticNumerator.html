Rotary Type Numerator (RTN)

Rotary Type Numerator (RTN)

The Rotary Type Numerator is a versatile tool for marking serial numbers and other data on metal parts. It features a manual wheel changing mechanism, allowing for precise control over the marking process.

Features

  • High-Quality Wheel Types: Made from premium tool steel for enhanced durability.
  • Manual Wheel Changing Mechanism: Provides flexibility in setting and changing markings.
  • Multi-Face Marking Wheels: Each wheel has 11 faces with figures 0 to 9 on ten faces, and one blank face.
  • Secure Locking Mechanism: Marking wheels can be rotated to any desired position and locked securely with a locking pin.
  • Compact and Sturdy: The unit is complete, compact, and ready to use without loose parts.
  • Durable Finish: All parts are specially heat-treated and nickel-plated for a dull finish.
  • High Performance: Built for an extended lifespan.
  • Customizable Combinations: Available in alpha-numeric and special wheel combinations to suit various needs.
  • Logo and Fixed Data Marking: Provision for marking logos or fixed letters along with numbers.
  • Special Wheels for Hot Stamping: Available upon request.
  • Customization for Different Press Types: Can be modified for use with impact, hydraulic, or fly presses.
  • Individual Wheel Release: Allows for the release of one wheel at a time without disturbing the setup of other wheels.

Automatic Numerator

Automatic Numerator

The Automatic Numerator is an efficient tool designed for marking serial numbers on metal parts through a simple operation. Its special design ensures that numbers advance accurately and automatically, one at a time, providing perfectly aligned, evenly spaced, and uniformly deep impressions on each component.

Features

  • High-Quality Type Wheels: Made from premium tool steel, ensuring durability and longevity.
  • Automatic Wheel Changing Mechanism: Provides seamless and automatic advancement of numbers.
  • Durable CAMS: Advancing CAMS are machined into solid wheels and made from the same durable material as the stamping characters.
  • Extended Wheel Life: Extra-wide unit wheels for prolonged usage.
  • Solid Actuating Pawl: One-piece actuating pawl machined from a solid bar for robustness.
  • Direct-Reading Characters: Facilitates easy setup and verification.
  • Ease of Maintenance: No riveted assembly allows for easy dismantling and cleaning by the user.
  • Unique Holding Catch and Springs: Designed for optimal performance.
  • Customizable Wheel Combinations: Available in alpha-numeric and special combinations to meet customer requirements.
  • High Performance: Built for longer life and reliable performance.
  • Special Features: Options for marking logos or fixed data along with serial numbers, as well as heater arrangements for hot foil stamping.
  • Custom Font Sizes: Available upon request.
