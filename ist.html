Interchangeable Steel Types

Our Steel Type sets are the perfect solution for marking serial and model numbers, alpha-numeric codes, and short phrases into steel and other metals with precision and durability. These sets are compatible with both hand and machine type holders, ensuring versatility for various stamping applications.

Features

  • Durability: Made from high-quality steel, our character sets are designed to withstand repeated use on tough surfaces, ensuring longevity and consistent results.
  • Compatibility: Specifically crafted to work seamlessly with Steel Type Holders, our sets are ideal for diverse marking tasks.
  • Variety: Available in various character sizes, our Steel Type sets cater to different marking needs, allowing you to choose the perfect size for your application.

Applications

  • Identification: Perfect for stamping permanent identification marks, our Steel Type sets are essential for maintaining traceability in manufacturing processes.
  • Custom Marking: Whether you need to mark model numbers, batch codes, or custom phrases, our sets provide the flexibility and precision required for detailed work.

Kit Composition

The Steel Type Kit is a comprehensive toolset designed for efficiency and convenience, comprising of 112 pieces, including:

  • Letters: Full alphabet in both upper and lower case.
  • Numbers: Complete set of digits from 0 to 9.
  • Symbols: Commonly used punctuation and special characters.
  • Holder: A sturdy steel type holder for both hand and machine use, ensuring precise alignment and consistent stamping results.

Benefits

  • Easy to Use: The ergonomic design of our type holders ensures comfortable handling and accurate stamping, reducing user fatigue during prolonged use.
  • Enhanced Traceability: With clear, legible marks, our sets help in maintaining accurate records and traceability of products, essential for quality control and regulatory compliance.
  • Customizable: Additional characters and custom symbols can be ordered to expand the versatility of your Steel Type Kit.
